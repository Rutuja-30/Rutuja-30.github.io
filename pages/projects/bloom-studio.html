Pressure- Breast Health Monitor Device

Overview

PresSure is an affordable at home breast wellness tool designed for women, especially those 30+ or with elevated personal or family history, who want more clarity between traditional self exams and infrequent clinical imaging.

The challenge

The existing brand had no cohesion: a generic logo, mismatched fonts across social and print, and no defined color palette. The goal was to unify everything and give the studio a voice it could grow into over the next decade.

🌸
📦

What I delivered

  • Primary and secondary logo suite
  • Custom color palette (5 colors with tints)
  • Typography system (2 font families)
  • Business cards, tissue paper, and kraft bag designs
  • Brand guidelines document (32 pages)

Outcome

The new brand launched in spring and was immediately noticed. Bloom Studio reported a 30% increase in social engagement within the first month, and several stockists reached out asking to carry their packaged products.
